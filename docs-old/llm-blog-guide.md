# Axite Blog Post Template (SEO + GEO + AI Visibility)

Format posts so the main answer is obvious in the first screen, then break the rest into skimmable sections with a clean heading hierarchy (one H1, then H2/H3) and quotable blocks that AI systems can extract.

## Recommended article template
- Title tag: descriptive and specific; avoid boilerplate and keyword stuffing; keep concise to prevent truncation.
- H1 (page title): one clear H1 that matches the core topic and is the most prominent on the page.
- Opening (2-5 sentences): answer-first summary of what the reader will learn, who it is for, and what is unique or original.
- Table of contents (optional): list H2 sections with jump links for long posts.
- Body sections (H2s): each H2 maps to a sub-question or sub-intent; start with a 1-2 sentence answer, then details.
- Subsections (H3s): steps, examples, edge cases, comparisons, and FAQs under the relevant H2.
- Proof + trust blocks: add citations, screenshots, data, or firsthand notes; include author byline and link to author/about page.
- FAQ (near end): 4-8 real questions with short, direct answers.
- Next step / CTA: one clear action so the page feels complete.

## Heading hierarchy rules (HTML)
- Use one h1 per page; make it the primary visible title.
- Use h2 for major sections and h3 for subsections; do not skip levels.
- Write headings as descriptive summaries, not generic labels.

## AI-friendly formatting patterns
- Put tight definitions in a 1-2 sentence paragraph directly under the relevant H2/H3.
- Prefer structured elements: numbered steps, bullets, and short paragraphs.
- Add a brief methodology note when you use tests, tools, or automation.

## Snippet-critical metadata
- Ensure every page has a unique title and avoid repeated boilerplate titles.
- Keep title text descriptive and concise; align title, H1, and prominent text.
