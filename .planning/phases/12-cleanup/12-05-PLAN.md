## Goal

Update the footer to remove links to deleted pages while keeping essential links (Trust, Terms, Privacy, Docs).

## Context

The current footer has 4 sections with many links that point to deleted pages:

Current structure:
- **Product**: Overview, Policy Enforcement, Identity & RBAC, Audit Trails, MCP Servers, Integrations, Pricing
- **Resources**: Documentation, Blog, Changelog, Support, System Status
- **Company**: About, Enterprise, Trust Center, Careers, Contact
- **Legal**: Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Privacy Settings, Security, DPA

For the landing page refactor, we need a minimal footer (LP-22): Docs link, Trust Center, Terms/Privacy, contact email.
